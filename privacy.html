Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit Ahmed Shehata.com

This happens through analytics tools like Google Analytics.

B) Information You Provide Voluntarily

If you fill out a contact form or send a message, we may collect:

You may :

We Use The Collected Data To :

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Protection

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to keep your data secure.

However, no method of internet transmission is 100% guaranteed.

6. Your Rights

To request changes, contact us using the information on the Contact page.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

The “Last Updated” date will always reflect the latest version.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through the Contact page on the website.
